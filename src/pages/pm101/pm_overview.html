1. What is Product Management?


Product Management (PM), also just called "Product", is a job function within technology that is typically responsible for owning a product or part of a product. At a small company or for a product leader, that may include a large suite of products. A big tech company or for more junior PM's


2. Why become a PM?


Product Management is relatively new job function that has grown exponentially in importance (and compensation) as the technology industry has grown close to 100x over the past 30 years. In 1996, all of the publicly traded software technology companies in the world represented less than 1% of the S&P 500 market capitalization in 1996 to 60%
