Interview Question: YouTube Shorts

You are PM for YouTube Shorts and notice that new videos aren't getting fairly distributed. Videos from big influencers go to followers and get picked up faster by the algorithms but videos from new creators aren't getting watched. You want to increase the explore part of the explore/exploit algorithm to find new creator content.

1. Assumptions

US only

2. Product Rationale

YouTube Mission: give everyone a voice and show them the world

Competition: TikTok, Reels

Product Mission: Find the best content from new creators and show it to the right audience at the right time

  • Encourage more content creators to create so that it creates a flywheel (More creators, more engagement, more content available, more viewers)
  • Aligns well with mission of giving everyone a voice; also helps show them the world and not just from perspective of big influencers
  • More sustainable business and better ecosystem vs big influencers are more transaction and will follow the money
  • Promoting new creators helps YouTube:
    • Differentiate from incumbents by offering better creator support.
    • Improve retention on both sides of the marketplace (creators and viewers).
    • Reduce dependency on a small cohort of superstar creators.

3. Entities

Creators

Viewer

Content

YouTube

4. Objectives

Find and promote new great content

  • Content: Understand what is in it and its quality
  • Viewer: Understand what type of content each user wants to see and when they want to see it
  • Creator: Understand if creator is new and high potential, normal user/viewer, one hit wonder

Guardrail:

  • Don't want to cannibalize content created from existing creators
  • Make sure we are exploring content that isn't divisive, unsafe, or presents a reputation risk
  • Reduce creator churn by giving new creators early traction, making content creation feel rewarding and sustainable.
  • Uncover breakout creators earlier to fuel long-term platform growth.

5. Potential Metrics

Joint

  • # of hours watched from new creators
  • % of total hours watched from new craters

Creator side

  • # new creators uploading Shorts
  • % new creators uploading again in 7/30 days
  • Churn rate of existing creators
  • Diversity of creators surfaced (Gini coefficient or entropy)
  • View-to-subscribe rate (for new creators)
  • # of new follows from new creators

Viewer side

  • % of viewers shown a new creator

6. North Star Metric

# of quality hours watched from new creators

  • Max dislikes of X%
  • Entire video watched

7. Leading indicators / Secondary Success Metrics

Precision and recall metrics for genre tagging

# of views required to get to stable expected % like, % dislike rates

8. Health / Guardrail Metrics

Total Video hours watched

  • So that we don't cannibalize existing creators

Regretted impressions: % of impressions that we show that end up not being successful

Individual video health from new creators vs existing

  • % likes
  • % dislikes
  • % Videos completed without a skip
  • % of videos that are shared
  • % of videos that get to follow
  • % of videos reported
